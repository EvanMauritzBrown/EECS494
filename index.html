Project 3

  • Project 3 (Unity)

Details

This is purrgatory, a metroidvania-style combat platformer where you play a cat on its journey through the Egyptian underworld.

It can be played alone or with a friend, bounce off walls and enemies to collect new abilities and collect the heart scarab.

I did a bit of everything on this project. I made art like the Anubis, Apep, soul orb, etc..., I did music such as the desert theme, Apep fight song, Necromancer fight song, etc...

Finally, I did a great deal in the codebase. I worked primarily on core systems like singlehandedly making the save/load data system. I also made the Apep boss, did jungle level design, new upgrades, ...

This was developed in Unity and C# with level design using LDtK.
